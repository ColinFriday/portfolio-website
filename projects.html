Projects

Whisp

Whisp is a 2D platformer video game that follows a spirit as it tries to collect orbs to gain new abilities. Implemented a proprietary physics engine and orb abilities. It was written in C# using Visual Studio and MonoGame. Worked on for Class, Team of 4, 3 Month Project.

Service Check

Service Check attempts to ping a specific IP in order to form a cheaper alternative to email out of the system if local power goes out and computers and switches go onto battery backup. Written in C#. Worked on for Main Line Accounting, Team of 1, 2 Week Project.

Curio Of Alexandria

Curio is a 2D puzzle game that follows a young archeologist as he explores an ancient tunnel and digs for artifacts. It is being written in C# using Visual Studio and MonoGame. Working on in personal time, Team of 1, Currently in progress.
