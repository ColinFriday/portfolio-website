Sports Broadcasting

I have been doing sports broadcasting and sports reporting for the past few years both at my high school and now at the college level. I am the Sports Director for WITR 89.7, a local Henrietta, NY radio station that is centered at RIT. I am also the Play By Play Announcer For RIT Women's Hockey. This experience has been second to none and I believe that I can do a fantastic job for any team that might be seeking either a color analyst or play by play broadcaster. I also run a weekly show called Drop The Puck on Friday mornings from 8-9 AM that covers Men's and Women's Hockey at RIT.
