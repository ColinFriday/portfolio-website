Welcome To My Portfolio!

Hi! My name is Colin Friday and welcome to my online portfolio! This site seeks to inform you about the many diverse interests I have and to explain my many qualifications both as a softwar/game developer and as a sports broadcaster. The page has been cut down into 7 distinct pages ranging from my emplyment experiences to my collegiate roller hockey career. The education and employment pages will be of great interest to any employer or recruiter as they include my academic accomplishments like my Presidential Scholarship at Rochester Institute of Technology as well as my accomplishments in the work place including high ranking positions such as Network Administrator for Main Line Accounting and Sports Director at WITR 89.7 FM. You are able to access a PDF version of my resume by clicking resume in the side navigation pane. If you interested in taking a look at my prowess in coding and game design please visit the projects page. The projects page contains listings and explanations of all my current and past projects so that you can get a feel for my style of work and coding. Additionally, the programming page contains a comprehensive list of all of the programming and other computing languages that I have used along with brief synopsis of my experience with each of them. Also, the external tools page gives an overview of all the external tools of which I am currently familiar. The final two pages are regarding my career with sports broadcasting and my career in collegiate roller hockey. these are both long standing hobbies of mine. Even though I realize thaat I am not skilled enough to play professional roller hockey, I do have interest in doing play-by-play or color commentary for a hockey team in the future.
